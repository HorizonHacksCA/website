GTOHacks 2024

A new hackathon for students in the GTA and across Canada.

What is GTOHacks?

  • A hackathon run by high school students, for high school students
  • We aim to introduce students to the world of programming and problem-solving and allow experienced students to use their skills to solve real-world problems
  • Each year, GTOHacks offers a new and innovative theme that challenges the participant's creativity and technical skills.

The Theme

For GTOHacks 2024, our theme will be studying. Participants will create a program to assist students in their studying. Key problems surrounding this topic may include procrastination, burnout, and information retention. In the name of fairness, a detailed case will be revealed on the first day so no team has an advantage.

Competition Format

  • 2 day format
  • Participants will use the first day to build teams and develop their ideas
  • The second day will be a cumulative presentation consisting of their vision, their process, and demonstration to our panel of judges.

Rules

As we strongly encourage the attendance of novices, this year's GTOHacks will be split into two distinct categories. Conceptual and Practical.

Conceptual

In the conceptual category, teams will be instructed to come up with a concept and an execution for a solution. This may look like making a slideshow consisting of their idea and how their idea will work. No working product for this category will be required and recommended for inexperienced teams who want to meet new people. All teams are welcome to attend the conceptual category.

Judging:

  • Concept: 50%
  • Presentation: 50%

Practical

The practical category will have teams put together actual working demos of their ideas in addition to giving presentations explaining to our panel of judges what their demos do. This category is recommended for advanced teams with experience in hackathons. All teams are welcome to attend the practical category.

Judging:

  • Concept: 25%
  • Presentation: 25%
  • Demo: 50%

Awards

Awards will be offered for the best overall project and best in each area of judging for both the Conceptual and Practical categories

  • Best Overall: Given to the team with the best overall presentation, concept, and execution
  • Best Concept: Given to the team deemed to have the best ideas that will have the most real-world impact
  • Best Presentation: Given to the team deemed to have the best understanding of how to execute their solutions in the real world
  • Best Demo (Practical only): Awarded to the team with the best demonstration/mockup of their ideas
*Please note that each team is only eligible for one award. Should a team win Best Overall, they will be immediately ineligible for any other awards. If a team is the second highest in all categories, they will be given the award of whichever category they have the highest score in, either Best Demo, Best Concept, or Best Execution.
